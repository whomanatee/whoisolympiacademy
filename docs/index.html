Public Persuasion

RALPH once noted that "the highest bribes of society are at the feet of the successful orator. All other fames must hush before his. He is the true potentate." Learn to persuade others and you will sit at the top of society. And there is enough room for everyone on the mountain top of persuasion. The only requirement to reach the summit of persuasion mountain is to seek truth above all else.

  1. persuading others of the truth, AKA rhetoric
  2. persuading others to use your business, AKA capitalism
  3. persuading others of a direction and means for success, AKA leadership
  4. persuading others by observing the nature of our reality, AKA science
  5. persuading others to break free from any tyranny or authority, AKA independence
  6. persuading others to grow without force or anger, AKA using wit

The Ailments of Society

Why do some suffer from mental and physical ailments, but others do not? It's a question that's been asked for thousands of years. We'll keep the conversation going with these topics.

  1. the study of non-conforming behavior, AKA personality disordering
  2. the study of setting boundaries, AKA anger
  3. the study of hiding the truth from others, AKA concealment
  4. the study of isolating from family and friends, AKA detachment
  5. the study of avoiding thinking of past wounds, AKA dissociation
  6. the study of going along with the desires of others, AKA agreeableness
  7. the study of doing things without caring of public perception, AKA disinhibition
  8. the study of phsyical pain without physical signs, AKA conversion disorder, PKA hysteria
